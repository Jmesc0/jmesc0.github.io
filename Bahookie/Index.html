Bahookie Soap

At Bahookie Soap, we are dedicated to elevating your self-care routine through a range of thoughtfully crafted products. Beyond our exquisite handmade soaps, we offer an array of accessories, from invigorating scrubs to fragrant candles, and even stylish accessories like soap dishes and loofahs. All are designed to complement your lifestyle while enhancing the ambiance of your daily toilette.

Crafting Moments of Bliss

Bahookie Soaps have been handcrafted with attention to quality and the desire for excellence. Our hope is to help you set the tone for your day by waking your senses in the morning and soothing them in the evening to help you relax.

Indulge in Variety

Bahookie offers you an immense selection of scented as well as unscented soaps. We also offer a large variety of fragrances in our scrubs that have been carefully crafted to bring out your skin's healthy glow! Hand poured, scented candles will further add to the overall ambience of the experience. ~ Don't be afraid to ask us to help you with a special order. We understand that special needs are a natural part of life and would love to help you if we can! Besides, it never hurts to ask!

Accessorize Your Routine

Along with our amazing soaps, we offer hand knit scrubbies, 3-D printed soap dishes and more!

Rooted in Quality

As a family-owned business, we take pride in being able to realize a dream while offering the most elegant skin care.

Community and Sustainability

Bahookie Soap is not just about products; it's about community and sustainability. We source locally whenever possible, supporting our community and minimizing our environmental footprint. Join us in the journey towards a more sustainable and indulgent self-care experience.

Experience the Difference

Explore the world of Bahookie Soap and indulge in a comprehensive range of skincare essentials. From the purity of our soaps to the indulgence of our candles and accessories, every product is crafted to elevate your self-care routine.

Thank You

...for choosing Bahookie Soap. Embrace the luxury of artisanal skincare and make every moment a celebration of self-care.
